# Project Vision

Jankalyan is a civic issue reporting platform designed to bridge the communication gap between citizens and government authorities.

The platform empowers citizens to report public infrastructure problems such as potholes, garbage accumulation, water leakage, streetlight failures, drainage issues, and other civic concerns in a structured, transparent, and trackable manner.

The long-term vision is to build a scalable digital civic engagement platform capable of integrating Artificial Intelligence, analytics, and government workflow automation.

---

# Mission

Enable citizens to:

- Report civic issues easily
- Track complaint progress
- Increase public participation
- Improve transparency
- Encourage faster resolution of public issues

---

# Problem Statement

Many civic issues remain unresolved because:

- Citizens do not know where to report.
- Reporting systems are complicated.
- Complaint tracking is poor.
- Duplicate reporting exists.
- Communication with authorities lacks transparency.

Jankalyan addresses these challenges through a centralized reporting platform.
