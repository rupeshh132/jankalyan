## PROJECT OVERVIEW

Jankalyan is a civic issue reporting platform where citizens can report public issues such as potholes, garbage, water leakage, damaged roads, streetlight failures, drainage issues, illegal dumping and other civic problems.

Citizens can:

- Register and login
- Report complaints
- Upload complaint images
- Track complaint status
- Vote on complaints
- Manage their profile

Administrators can:

- Review complaints
- Approve or reject complaints
- Update complaint status
- Manage complaint categories
- Access analytics dashboard

Future versions may include AI-generated government letters and duplicate complaint detection.
